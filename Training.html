About Us

Training

Welcome to our training section. In this section we will talk to you on how our sessions work and explain you the cost of the training. Be asure that when you finisshed these sessions you will be able to drive our exclusive vehicles.

LAV-25

The first vehicle you will have the opportunity to drive is the LAV-25. It is an amphibious light-armored vehicle equipped with eight wheels. It weighs approximatly 12.8 tonnes but is able to reach 100km/h or 62 mp/h.

M113

The second tank you will have the opportunity to drive is the M113 Armored Personnal Carrier. It was used in the 1960's to present days. The version we currently are using was made in 1975. It weighs 12 tonnes, goes up to 67.6 km/h, or 42 mp/h. It is also amphibious. It was made to carry up to 11 passengers and 2 crew members.

T34-85

Last but not least is the T34-85. The beast of the battlefield was produced in 1940 during WW2, in soviet Russia. The very well perfoming tank served during the last year of the great war, before being sold to us for refurbishing reasons. The noew fully fonctionnal front turret will be available for you to shoot during the training day.
